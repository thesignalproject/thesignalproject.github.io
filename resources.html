Stand on the Shoulders of Giants

Seeking out the knowledge of successful individuals is crucial. It's what a lot of the self-help genre refers to as "modeling". Look to the people who have already achieved the results you want, most successful people share the strategies they have used to get to where they are today. It's impossible to figure everything out yourself. You will necessarily have to rely on the advice of others. I've tried to collect together the resources and websites that consistently get me to think bigger and better.
